Shift Clock is an advanced alarm clock app designed to solve the challenges of rotating schedules and irregular shifts.

Conventional alarm apps assume you work Monday to Friday. Shift Clock allows you to create alarms based on your work cycles (e.g., 2 work days and 3 days off), regardless of the days of the week.

Ideal for healthcare workers, pilots, rescue and emergency responders, military, and any shift worker.

Key Features:
- Cyclic Pattern Alarms: Calculates your work schedule automatically.
- Gentle Wake: Volume increases gradually.
- Stopwatch and Timer with insistent alarms.
- Completely free and open-source (GPLv3).
